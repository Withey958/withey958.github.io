Luke Withey

Product Development in Norwich, UK

Developing products for humans in the physical, digital and data dimensions.

Users have always been at the core of any product's success and should be at the core of any engineer's design decision process. Products can no longer solely rely on great hardware to create an impact, they also have to be powered by intelligent data usage and digital interfaces and architectures to achieve their goals. These facts have driven my professional development towards becoming a T-shaped engineer.

I have been involved in Product Development for almost a decade managing products' journey from idea to mass production. With experience in the health care, well being, consumer, automotive, OEM and industrial markets.

In my spare time I enjoy volleyball, snowboarding, running the Crumpetorium with my extremely talented partner and tinkering on extracurricular projects.

I have returned to the UK after a 6 month trip around South America and Oceania. I am extremely keen to start creating products again, if you would like to talk please get in touch.
